About Tahleb

Hello! I’m Tahleb, a passionate wildlife photographer born in the picturesque town of Narok and raised amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Maasai Mara.

Growing up surrounded by the untamed beauty of nature and the majestic wildlife, I developed a deep appreciation for the natural world. My journey into photography began as a way to capture and share the awe-inspiring moments I witnessed daily—from the graceful stride of a lioness to the vibrant hues of a sunset over the wild.

Through my lens, I strive to bring the beauty of wildlife to life, capturing the essence of each creature and the environment they inhabit. My work is not just about taking pictures; it’s about preserving the magic of nature and inspiring others to appreciate and protect our planet’s incredible biodiversity.

Join me on this visual journey and explore the captivating world of wildlife through my eyes.
